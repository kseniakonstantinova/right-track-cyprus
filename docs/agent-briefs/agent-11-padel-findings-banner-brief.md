# Padel Findings Banner Brief

## Goal
Create a new carousel banner for the Community page that replaces the old padel survey slide.

This banner should promote the new findings article, not the survey form.

## Use Existing Visual Base
Use the same male padel player from the old survey banner:

- source image: `/Users/user/Right Track/assets/images/banners/padel-research.jpg`
- alternate format: `/Users/user/Right Track/assets/images/banners/padel-research.webp`

Do not switch to a different athlete or a different visual style unless explicitly approved.

## Asset Intent
The banner should communicate:

- local community insight
- pain and injury are common in padel
- the content is based on responses from 50+ players
- the next step is to read the findings

This is not:

- an academic paper cover
- an app advertisement
- a clinic promo banner
- a generic stock-sports visual

## Core Message
Primary angle:

- `87% reported pain or injury in the last 12 months`

Supporting context:

- `Based on responses from 50+ padel players in Cyprus`

Optional secondary angle:

- `Most kept playing through it`

## Visual Direction
Keep the current player image and build the banner around it.

Recommended composition:

- player stays on the right side
- left side remains darker and cleaner for text
- maintain strong contrast for readability
- preserve the night-court / premium-sport look

Suggested treatment:

- deepen the left-side overlay slightly for text legibility
- optionally add a subtle amber/orange accent glow or stat chip
- avoid heavy infographic styling
- avoid cluttering the image with too many numbers

## Optional Stat Chips
If the design supports small stat chips, keep it to max 2:

- `87% pain or injury`
- `50+ Cyprus players`

Do not add a crowded dashboard.

## Constraints
- keep the same athlete from the old banner
- keep the image photorealistic
- no fake charts
- no medical icons overload
- no app-store/product UI mockup
- no overclaiming language such as `study proves`
- no more than one primary stat on the banner face
