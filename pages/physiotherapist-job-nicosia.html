What You'll Actually Do

This is a bridge role: broad clinical responsibility now, with growth into sports and performance over time. We treat athletes, office workers, post-surgical patients, elderly, and neurological cases — and so will you.

In-Clinic Rehabilitation

Assess and treat patients at our Strovolos clinic. MSK conditions, post-surgical rehab, chronic pain, and sports injuries. Hands-on treatment with exercise-based programming.

Homecare Visits

Travel to patients across Nicosia for home-based physiotherapy. Elderly patients, neurological conditions, post-operative recovery. You work independently and manage your own schedule.

Sports & MSK Caseload

Treat athletes and active patients returning from injury. Shoulder, knee, ankle, and spinal conditions. Contribute to return-to-sport programming and performance outcomes.

Elderly & Neurological Care

Stroke recovery, Parkinson's, age-related decline, fall prevention. These patients require patience, consistency, and genuine care. This is not optional work — it is core to the role.

Who This Role Is For

We're looking for a specific kind of clinician. Read both lists carefully before applying.

This role is for you if...

  • You are comfortable treating a mixed caseload — athletes, elderly, post-surgical, neurological
  • You are willing to do homecare visits and work independently outside the clinic
  • You want to grow into sports rehab and performance, and you're willing to earn that growth
  • You take ownership of your patients and their outcomes
  • You are actively working to improve as a clinician — courses, reading, mentorship
  • You want to work in a clinic that is building something serious, not just filling appointment slots

This role is not for you if...

  • You only want to treat athletes and will not engage with elderly or homecare patients
  • You prefer passive treatment approaches — ultrasound, hot packs, minimal patient engagement
  • You want a low-responsibility position where you clock in and clock out
  • You are looking for a guaranteed sports physio title without doing the mixed-caseload work first
  • You are not willing to travel for homecare visits across Nicosia

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a sports-only physiotherapy role?
No. This is a mixed-caseload role covering clinic-based rehabilitation, homecare visits, and sports/MSK cases. Right Track is building toward a stronger sports rehab identity, and the right clinician will grow into that direction over time.
Do I need to be GESY-registered to apply?
You need to be licensed or eligible to practise physiotherapy in Cyprus. GESY registration is a plus but can be arranged after hiring if you meet the licensing requirements.
What kind of growth can I expect?
Right Track is actively investing in clinician development and visibility. The clinic is building a sports and performance identity, and the right hire will have opportunities to grow into sports rehab, contribute to educational content, and develop professional visibility within the brand.
Do I need my own transport?
A driver's licence and access to a vehicle are strongly preferred, as homecare visits require travel across Nicosia.
