⚠️ STOP LIST — WHAT NOT TO DO

🚫 DO NOT buy followers

Kills algorithmic reach. Dead followers = your content isn’t shown to real people.

🚫 DO NOT post without strategy

Every post = a step in the funnel. “Just post regularly” is not a strategy, it’s a hope.

🚫 DO NOT copy competitors

Copying = position #2 forever. We create our own category: evidence-based sports rehab.

🚫 DO NOT run ads without a foundation

Ads without content = wasted budget. 3 months of organic first, then scale with ads.

🚫 DO NOT use stock photos

In 2026 authenticity = currency. Real clinic, real Antonis, real patients (with consent).

🚫 DO NOT be “for everyone”

Focus: athletes and active people. Narrow the positioning, expand reach through expertise.
