Costs and Sessions

These are the core facts most patients need before booking.

Co-payment
€10 /session

GESY physiotherapy usually involves a small co-payment, with an annual cap of €150 or €75 for pensioners and children under 21.

Typical allocation
Up to 12

Musculoskeletal conditions typically receive up to 12 sessions per referral. Post-surgical cases can receive up to 24 sessions.

Home visits
GESY +€15

GESY also covers physiotherapy at home. A €15 travel surcharge applies per visit on top of the standard co-payment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept GESY referrals at Right Track?
Yes. Right Track accepts GESY physiotherapy referrals in Nicosia.
Do I need a referral before booking?
For GESY treatment, yes. You need a referral from your GESY GP or specialist. If you want to start privately, you can book directly without a referral.
Can I choose which physiotherapist I see?
Yes. Under GESY, you can choose any registered physiotherapist. At Right Track, Antonis Petri (A2825) and Alice Kazanjian (A3509) are registered providers.
How many sessions will I get?
That depends on the condition. Musculoskeletal cases typically receive up to 12 sessions per referral, while post-surgical cases may receive up to 24. Extensions can be requested when clinically justified.
What should I bring to the first appointment?
Bring your referral details, any relevant medical reports or imaging, and wear comfortable clothing that allows movement.
