Frequently Asked Questions

Which areas do you cover?
We cover the greater Nicosia area including Strovolos, Lakatamia, Engomi, Aglantzia, Latsia, and surrounding areas. For locations further afield, please contact us to discuss availability.
How does GESY homecare work?
Your doctor provides a referral for homecare physiotherapy. Treatment sessions are covered by GESY, with a €15 travel surcharge per visit. This applies to patients who are medically unable to travel to the clinic.
What equipment do you bring?
We bring everything needed for a full treatment session including assessment tools, treatment aids, exercise equipment, and therapeutic modalities. You don't need to provide anything.
How long is a home session?
Sessions typically last 45-60 minutes, including assessment, treatment, and exercise instruction. The first session may be slightly longer as it includes a comprehensive initial evaluation.
Can you treat post-surgical patients at home?
Absolutely. Home-based post-surgical rehabilitation is one of our core services. We work with patients recovering from joint replacements, spinal surgery, and fracture fixation, starting treatment as soon as medically appropriate.
