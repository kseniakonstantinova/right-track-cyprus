Your First Visit

What to expect when your physiotherapist arrives at your door.

Duration: 45-60 Min

Your first home session is a full-length appointment. We spend time on a thorough assessment and still begin hands-on treatment — no wasted visits.

What to Prepare

Have comfortable clothing ready, any medical reports or imaging (X-rays, MRIs), and a clear space of roughly 2×2 metres. We bring everything else.

Health & Injury History

We review your medical background, current symptoms, previous treatments, activity level, and goals. The more detail you share, the faster we can target the right approach.

Physical Assessment

Functional movement screening, range of motion testing, strength assessment, and special orthopaedic tests — all performed in your home with portable equipment.

Treatment Starts Day One

We don't wait for a second visit to begin treatment. Manual therapy, initial exercises, and pain management strategies start during your first session.

Home Exercise Program

You leave the first session with a personalised exercise plan designed specifically for your home environment — no gym required. Clear instructions and progressions included.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which areas do you cover?
We cover the greater Nicosia area including Strovolos, Lakatamia, Engomi, Aglantzia, Latsia, and surrounding areas. For locations further afield, please contact us to discuss availability.
Can I train while doing home rehab?
In most cases, yes. We design rehab programs that work alongside your training. We'll modify your load, adjust exercises, and give you clear guidelines on what you can and can't do — so you stay active while recovering safely.
What equipment do you bring?
Everything needed for a full session: resistance bands, assessment tools, manual therapy aids, and portable exercise equipment. For sport-specific rehab, we may also bring agility and functional testing tools.
How long is a home session?
Sessions typically last 45-60 minutes, including hands-on treatment, exercise prescription, and sport-specific drills. The first session may be slightly longer as it includes a comprehensive assessment.
How does GESY homecare work?
Your doctor provides a referral for homecare physiotherapy. Treatment sessions are covered by GESY, with a €15 travel surcharge per visit. This applies to patients who are medically unable to travel to the clinic.
Is home physiotherapy as effective as clinic-based treatment?
Yes. Research shows that home-based physiotherapy achieves comparable outcomes to clinic-based care for most conditions. In some cases, it can be more effective because exercises are practised in the environment where you'll actually use them, and adherence tends to be higher when sessions are more convenient.
How much space do I need at home?
A clear area of roughly 2×2 metres is usually enough for a full session. We adapt to your space — whether it's a living room, bedroom, or garden. We bring a portable treatment couch and all necessary equipment, so you don't need to prepare anything special.
Can I combine home visits with clinic sessions?
Absolutely. Many patients start with home visits during the early recovery phase and transition to clinic sessions as their mobility improves. We also offer a blended approach where you alternate between home and clinic based on your weekly schedule and treatment needs.
Do you treat non-sports injuries at home?
Yes. While we specialise in sports and active populations, our home visits also cover general musculoskeletal conditions — back pain, neck pain, post-surgical rehab from joint replacements, and age-related mobility issues. The same clinical standards apply regardless of your condition.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or changes. This allows us to offer the slot to another patient. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. You can reschedule easily by phone or through our online booking system.
