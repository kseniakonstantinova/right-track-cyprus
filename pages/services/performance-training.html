Frequently Asked Questions

What sports do you train for?
We design programs for all sports including football, basketball, volleyball, padel, martial arts, running, swimming, cycling, and more. Each program is tailored to the specific physical demands of your sport.
Is the gym included in the monthly program?
Yes. The monthly program includes full access to our on-site gym during operating hours, plus supervised training sessions and program design. Everything you need is included.
How are programs designed?
Programs are based on your initial physical assessment, sport demands, competition schedule, and goals. We use periodization principles to structure training phases and adjust based on monthly reassessments.
Can I combine performance training with physiotherapy?
Absolutely. Many athletes use both services, especially during return-to-play. Our team coordinates physiotherapy treatment with your training program for a seamless transition from rehab to performance.
What equipment is available?
Our gym is equipped with free weights, resistance machines, plyometric equipment, agility tools, and assessment devices. It's designed as a clinical training environment — everything an athlete needs for performance development.
