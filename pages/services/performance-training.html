Our Approach

How we develop athletes — from assessment to peak performance.

Periodised Programming

Training is structured in phases — off-season base building, pre-season intensity, in-season maintenance, and active recovery. Every block has a purpose aligned with your competition calendar.

Data-Driven Progression

Monthly physical assessments track strength, power, speed, and movement quality. We adjust your program based on objective data, not guesswork — so every session moves you forward.

Physiotherapist-Led Training

Your trainer is a qualified physiotherapist. This means injury risk is managed during every session, load is prescribed safely, and your training integrates seamlessly with any rehab work.

Sport-Specific Movement Patterns

We don't just train muscles — we train movements. Cutting, jumping, decelerating, rotational power — exercises are selected to directly transfer to your sport's demands on the field or court.

Load Management

Training load is coordinated with your team practice and competition schedule. We monitor total weekly load to maximise adaptation while minimising injury risk — especially during heavy match periods.

Clinical Training Environment

Our gym is purpose-built for athletic development — not a commercial fitness centre. Free weights, plyometric equipment, agility tools, and assessment technology in a focused, professional setting.

Your First Session

What to expect when you start performance training at Right Track.

Duration: 60-75 Min

Your first session is a comprehensive assessment. We take extra time to understand your athletic background, test your current physical capacity, and design your initial program.

What to Bring

Athletic clothing, training shoes, and a water bottle. If you have any recent medical reports, injury history documents, or performance data from your team, bring those too.

Physical Profiling

We test strength, power, speed, agility, flexibility, and movement quality. This baseline data drives your entire program and gives us clear metrics to measure progress against.

Sport & Schedule Analysis

We discuss your sport, position, competition calendar, team training schedule, and personal goals. Training is built around your existing commitments — not the other way around.

Program Design

Based on your assessment, you receive a personalised monthly training program. This includes exercise selection, sets, reps, load targets, and progression criteria — all accessible digitally.

Training Starts Immediately

After the assessment, you'll complete your first supervised training session. We don't wait — you leave day one with a program in hand and the first workout done.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sports do you train for?
We design programs for all sports including football, basketball, volleyball, padel, martial arts, running, swimming, cycling, and more. Each program is tailored to the specific physical demands of your sport.
Is the gym included in the monthly program?
Yes. The monthly program includes full access to our on-site gym during operating hours, plus supervised training sessions and program design. Everything you need is included.
How are programs designed?
Programs are based on your initial physical assessment, sport demands, competition schedule, and goals. We use periodization principles to structure training phases and adjust based on monthly reassessments.
Can I combine performance training with physiotherapy?
Absolutely. Many athletes use both services, especially during return-to-play. Our team coordinates physiotherapy treatment with your training program for a seamless transition from rehab to performance.
What equipment is available?
Our gym is equipped with free weights, resistance machines, plyometric equipment, agility tools, and assessment devices. It's designed as a clinical training environment — everything an athlete needs for performance development.
Do I need to be an elite athlete to join?
Not at all. We work with athletes at every level — from recreational runners and weekend footballers to competitive and semi-professional athletes. Programs are scaled to your current fitness and goals. What matters is commitment, not your current level.
How often should I train?
This depends on your sport, competition schedule, and goals. Most athletes on our monthly program train 2-4 times per week at our gym. We design around your team training and match schedule so total load remains optimal without overtraining.
What happens during monthly reassessments?
We re-test key metrics from your initial assessment — strength, power, speed, and movement quality. We compare results, discuss how training felt, review any niggles, and adjust your next month's program accordingly. It takes about 30 minutes and is included in the monthly fee.
Can I train at the gym outside of supervised sessions?
Yes. The monthly program includes full gym access during operating hours. You'll have your program on your phone and can complete sessions independently. Supervised sessions ensure technique is correct and progressions are on track.
What age groups do you work with?
We train athletes from age 14 upwards. For younger athletes, programs focus on movement fundamentals, coordination, and safe strength development. For adults, we address sport-specific performance, injury prevention, and competitive readiness.
Where is your gym located?
Our gym and clinic is at Tseriou 32, Strovolos, Nicosia. Centrally located and easy to reach from Engomi, Aglantzia, Latsia, Makedonitissa, and Lakatamia. Street parking available nearby.
