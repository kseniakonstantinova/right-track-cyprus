Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

2. Cookies We Use

Right Track Physiotherapy & Performance Centre uses a minimal number of cookies:

Cookie Purpose Type Duration
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics 4 — anonymous visitor statistics (pages visited, time on site, country) Analytics 2 years
cookie_consent Remembers your cookie preference (accept/reject) Essential (localStorage) Persistent
preferred_language Remembers your language preference (English/Greek) Essential (localStorage) Persistent

3. Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our website. GA4 collects anonymous, aggregated data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Country and city (approximate, not your exact location)
  • Device type and browser
  • How you found our website (search engine, social media, direct)

GA4 does not collect your name, email, phone number, or any health-related data. We use this data solely to improve our website and services.

Analytics cookies are only loaded after you accept cookies via our consent banner. If you reject cookies, no analytics data is collected.

4. Essential Storage

We use your browser's localStorage (not cookies) to remember:

  • Your cookie consent choice (so the banner doesn't reappear)
  • Your language preference (English or Greek)

This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

5. How to Manage Cookies

You can manage cookies in several ways:

  • Our consent banner: Accept or reject analytics cookies when you first visit the site
  • Browser settings: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Check your browser's help section for instructions
  • Clear localStorage: Open your browser's developer tools (F12) → Application → Local Storage → clear the entries for our domain

6. Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics may set its own cookies. Google's privacy policy applies to these: Google Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or social media cookies.
