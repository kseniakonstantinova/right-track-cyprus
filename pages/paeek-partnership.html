Official Partnership

Pitch-Side Physiotherapy for PAEEK Academies

Right Track provides match-day medical coverage, injury monitoring, and athlete care for PAEEK Academies & Dragon Force — FC Porto Academy Cyprus.

What We Do

As the officially registered physiotherapists for PAEEK Academies, Antonis Petri and the Right Track team are present on match days and during training sessions to ensure athletes receive immediate, professional care when it matters most.

Match-Day Coverage

Pitch-side presence during competitive matches, providing immediate injury assessment and on-field first response.

Injury Monitoring

Overseeing and monitoring athletes for injuries during matches and training, identifying risks before they become serious problems.

On-Field Assessment

Rapid evaluation of acute injuries on the pitch, making return-to-play decisions and providing immediate treatment when needed.

Athlete Rehabilitation

Supporting injured players through structured rehabilitation programmes to ensure a safe and timely return to competition.

About the Partnership

PAEEK Academies has formed a strategic partnership with Dragon Force, the official international football academy of FC Porto, expanding elite football development to Cyprus.

Antonis Petri of Right Track Physiotherapy is the officially registered physiotherapist for the joint club, bringing professional sports physiotherapy standards to every training session and match day.
