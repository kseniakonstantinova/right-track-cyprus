3. Individual Variation & Risk

Health conditions, injuries, and recovery timelines vary significantly between individuals. Information presented in our articles is based on published research and general clinical experience, but may not be appropriate for your specific situation.

Factors that can affect the applicability of general information include:

  • Pre-existing medical conditions or comorbidities
  • Previous injuries and surgical history
  • Current medications
  • Age, fitness level, and activity demands
  • Individual anatomical and biomechanical differences

4. Exercise & Activity Disclaimer

Any exercises, stretches, movement protocols, or physical activity recommendations described on this website are general in nature. Performing exercises without proper individual assessment carries inherent risk of injury.

Before beginning any exercise programme described on this website:

  • Consult your physiotherapist, physician, or qualified healthcare provider
  • Obtain an individual assessment if you have any existing pain, injury, or medical condition
  • Stop immediately and seek professional advice if you experience pain, dizziness, or unusual symptoms during any exercise

You perform any exercises described on this website entirely at your own risk.
