1. Introduction

Right Track Rehabilitation & Performance Centre ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Cyprus data protection laws.

3. Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address
  • Appointment Data: Booking dates, times, service type, therapist preference
  • Health Information: Medical history, treatment notes, diagnosis (with your explicit consent)
  • Payment Information: Payment type (GESY/Private), transaction records
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type (collected automatically via cookies)

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To schedule and manage your appointments
  • To provide physiotherapy and rehabilitation services
  • To communicate with you about your treatment
  • To process payments and maintain financial records
  • To comply with GESY (National Health System) requirements
  • To improve our services
  • To send appointment reminders (with your consent)

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contract: To provide the services you have requested
  • Consent: For marketing communications and processing health data
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with healthcare regulations and tax laws
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and ensure security

6. Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • GESY/HIO: For patients using the national health system
  • Service Providers: Google Firebase (data storage), EmailJS (notifications)
  • Healthcare Professionals: When referrals are necessary (with your consent)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for:

  • Medical Records: 10 years (as required by Cyprus healthcare regulations)
  • Booking Records: 3 years
  • Financial Records: 7 years (tax requirements)

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw Consent: At any time for consent-based processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at antonis@righttrackphysio.com.cy

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure cloud storage (Google Firebase with EU data centers)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

10. Cookies

We use cookies for essential website functionality and analytics. See our Cookie Policy for details.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or website notice.

12. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you may contact:

  • Us directly at antonis@righttrackphysio.com.cy
